Services

Our packages are flexible so you can add and take away fillers as you wish and if there is something which you would like in there which we do not supply, please do speak to us and we will be happy to try and accommodate your request. We currently stock sweet boxes which are a great alternative to our hampers, we can have up to three different fillers in each box. Our fillers are also available as favours, they are perfect for weddings, birthdays and baby showers. Please contact us to discuss your needs.

We sell our hamper fillers as a part of a package and also individually, we currently stock four sizes of hampers: small, medium, large, and extra large.
